Software Projects

We love bringing ideas to life. We provide a team of craftsmen to deliver an on-going or fixed scope piece of work. Idealy a team would be composed by 3 to 5 of our craftsmen and a Product Owner from your company. We will manage the day-to-day running of the team and can run the project on our own premises if required.

Consulting

We provide specialised advice if you are starting a new project or if quality of software and teams need considerable improvements. We can work with you for a few days, giving you and your teams guidance, or for a longer period of time embedded in your teams, helping developers and all people involved to master their practices.

Training

We love to share what we know. We help teams to get better at Test-Driven Development, Continuous Integration and Deployment, Domain-Driven Design, Clean Code and SOLID principles, Behaviour Driven Development, and many other technical practices. We help teams in any phase in the project where the they may benefit from focused training.
